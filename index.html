Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. Extensibility with plugins themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With functionality, you're in full control.
